About Bharadwaj Ayurvedic Clinic

Founded in 1995, Bharadwaj Ayurvedic Clinic has been serving the community with authentic Ayurvedic treatments and remedies. Our mission is to bring the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda to modern life, helping people achieve optimal health naturally.

Dr. Rajesh Bharadwaj, our chief Ayurvedic practitioner, has over 25 years of experience and comes from a long lineage of Ayurvedic physicians. He combines traditional knowledge with a contemporary approach to healthcare.

We believe in treating the root cause of diseases rather than just the symptoms, ensuring long-lasting health and wellness for our patients.
